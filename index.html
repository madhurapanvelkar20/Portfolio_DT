About

Eight years of bridging theory and real-world AI

I am an Assistant Professor at Symbiosis Institute of Technology (SIT), Pune, where I teach and mentor students in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Computer Vision. My doctoral research focused on multi-view video summarization using deep learning — developing intelligent frameworks for query-based video analysis.

Before joining SIT, I spent over 8 years at Bharati Vidyapeeth Deemed University, where I taught C Programming, Java, Python, Data Science, Digital Communication, and Image Processing — while coordinating the department's Research & Development initiatives.

My teaching philosophy blends rigorous fundamentals with hands-on, industry-ready application. I believe technology education must be grounded in empathy, curiosity, and real-world impact.
