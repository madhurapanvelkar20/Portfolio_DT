About

A decade of turning curiosity into impact

Professor of Design Thinking with over 12 years of experience bridging academia and industry. My work is rooted in the belief that the best solutions emerge when deep empathy meets disciplined creativity.

I teach, mentor, research, and consult — working with students, founders, and corporations to embed human-centered design as a core competency for the modern world.
